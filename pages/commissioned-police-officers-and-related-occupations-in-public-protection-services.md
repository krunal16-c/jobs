## Description

Commissioned police officers and related occupations in public protection services is a Canadian occupation classified under **Education, law and social services** in the National Occupational Classification (NOC) 2021 system, with NOC code 40040.

These occupations include professional roles in law, education, social work, community services, and government.

Workers in this occupation are classified at **TEER 4**: Occupations that usually require a secondary school diploma or several weeks of on-the-job training.
