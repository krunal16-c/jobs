## Description

Senior managers - financial, communications and other business services is a Canadian occupation classified under **Management occupations** in the National Occupational Classification (NOC) 2021 system, with NOC code 00012.

These occupations involve planning, organizing, directing, controlling and evaluating various aspects of business and government organizations.

Workers in this occupation are classified at **TEER 0**: Management occupations. No formal education required; typically acquired through extensive experience.
