## Description

Veterinarians is a Canadian occupation classified under **Health occupations** in the National Occupational Classification (NOC) 2021 system, with NOC code 31103.

These occupations involve diagnosing and treating illness and injury and promoting health, including professional, technical, and assisting roles in health care.

Workers in this occupation are classified at **TEER 3**: Occupations that usually require a college diploma, an apprenticeship training of less than two years, or more than six months of on-the-job training.
