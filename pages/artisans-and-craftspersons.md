## Description

Artisans and craftspersons is a Canadian occupation classified under **Art, culture, recreation and sport** in the National Occupational Classification (NOC) 2021 system, with NOC code 53124.

These occupations include professional, technical, and general roles in the arts, culture, recreation, and sport.

Workers in this occupation are classified at **TEER 5**: Occupations that usually require short-term work demonstration or on-the-job training.
