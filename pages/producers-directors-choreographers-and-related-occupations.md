Producers, directors, choreographers and related occupations is a Canadian occupation classified under **Art, culture, recreation and sport** in the National Occupational Classification (NOC) 2021 system, with NOC code 51120.

These occupations include professional, technical, and general roles in the arts, culture, recreation, and sport.
