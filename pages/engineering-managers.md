## Description

Engineering managers is a Canadian occupation classified under **Natural and applied sciences** in the National Occupational Classification (NOC) 2021 system, with NOC code 20010.

These occupations include professional and technical roles in physical sciences, life sciences, computer science, engineering, and architecture.

Workers in this occupation are classified at **TEER 2**: Occupations that usually require a college diploma or apprenticeship training of two or more years, or supervisory occupations.
